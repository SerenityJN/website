Step-by-Step Registration Procedure

Applicant Steps

  1. Open the registration page.
  2. Create an account with email, mobile number, and password.
  3. Verify your contact (email link or mobile OTP).
  4. Fill in personal information.
  5. Choose academic options (strand, grade level, schedule).
  6. Upload required documents.
  7. Review all information before submission.
  8. Pay fees (if applicable).
  9. Submit application and receive a reference number.
  10. Wait for confirmation via email/SMS.

Admin / Back-Office Steps

  1. System validates required fields and uploads.
  2. Staff review submitted documents.
  3. Allocate strand slots and notify applicants.
  4. Schedule exams or interviews.
  5. Approve accepted students and issue enrollment number.
  6. Confirm payment and issue receipt.
  7. Send orientation details.
  8. Archive documents and generate reports.

Validation Rules & Policies

  • Email, mobile, password, and personal info are required.
  • Password must be at least 8 characters with letters and numbers.
  • Mobile must be 11 digits starting with 09.
  • File uploads must be JPG, PNG, or PDF (max 5MB).
  • Applicants must agree to Terms & Privacy Policy.

Notifications

  • Verification email / OTP message.
  • Submission receipt with reference number.
  • Document request notice if files are missing.
  • Decision notice (accepted, waitlisted, or rejected).
  • Payment receipt and reminders for exams or orientation.
